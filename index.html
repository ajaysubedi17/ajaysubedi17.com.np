About

Civil and Environmental Engineer based in Kathmandu, Nepal, with M.Sc. in Environmental Engineering from Tribhuvan University's Institute of Engineering, Pulchowk Campus.

My work sits at the intersection of environmental systems and machine learning. I build and apply ML models to predict, optimize, and solve problems in solid waste management, water quality, wastewater treatment, and environmental impact — domains where data-driven approaches are still underutilised.

Beyond research, I believe in closing the gap between computational methods and engineering practice — through teaching, tutorials, and tools that make these approaches accessible to those who are interested.
